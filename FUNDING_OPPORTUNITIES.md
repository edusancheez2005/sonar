## ⚠️ Regulatory Note

Because Sonar produces "actionable trading signals" and an AI advisor:

- The **FCA** has detailed expectations for cryptoasset financial promotions and is progressing a new crypto regulatory regime (authorisation window starting 30 Sept 2026).
- If product outputs become "personal recommendations", the FCA perimeter guidance applies even to standardised communications.
- **Fundraising implication:** Position Sonar as "market intelligence + research tooling" (with clear disclaimers) rather than "investment advice", and show a plan to comply with UK crypto marketing rules.
